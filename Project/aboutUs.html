About Us:

La Cucaracha Pizzeria is a vibrant and passionate local pizza spot founded by three dedicated team members — Niv, Yuval, and Oren. With a shared love for great food and community spirit, they came together to create a place where everyone can enjoy fresh, handcrafted pizzas made with high-quality ingredients and lots of heart. From classic favorites to creative topping combos, La Cucaracha blends flavor and fun in every bite, delivering not just meals, but memorable experiences. Whether you’re dining in or grabbing a slice on the go, the team at La Cucaracha is here to make sure you feel right at home.
